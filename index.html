Introduction
In this project, I visualized the Korean population pyramid model in my own way
to represent the burden on the working-age population due to the country's rapid aging.
I visualized the Korean population pyramid model in my own way,
adding special events along the way as a warning for the future.
Guide
Tap the homepage banner to see what the population model looks like over the years.
When you first view the artwork, it's helpful to view it in chronological order.
Please switch to full screen and scale to 100%. (Optimized for Chrome browser.)
This project is optimized for 1440 x 788 screens, so it may look different on other screens.
